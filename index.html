About

Situated along the banks of the east branch of the perkiomen creek, Country Creek Winery has been family owned and operated since 1994.
We produce a variety of Pennsylvania wines: Concord, Niagara, Cayuga, Merlot, Cabernet Sauvignon, and seasonal blueberry, strawberry, apple, and blackberry.
Our main location is in Salford Township and our second location is in Souderton inside Frederick's Flowers. You can also find us at the Night Market in Telford, which runs every Wednesday | 5:30 PM - 8:30 PM | June to September.
Contact us to find out more about our music events and workshops or to schedule private parties, weddings, or showers.

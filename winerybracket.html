About

Situated along the banks of the east branch of the perkiomen creek, Country Creek Winery has been family owned and operated since 1994. We produce a variety of Pennsylvania wines: Concord, Niagara, Cayuga, Merlot, Cabernet Sauvignon, and seasonal blueberry, strawberry, apple, and blackberry. Our main location is in Salford Township and our second location is in Souderton inside Frederick's Flowers.
